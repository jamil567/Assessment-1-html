Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe is easy to make and results in delicious cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 & 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Combine melted butter with brown and granulated sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  4. Add flour and baking soda, then mix until well combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Shape the dough into small circles.
  7. Place the dough circles on a baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 18 minutes.
  9. Let the cookies cool before serving. Enjoy!
