God of War: Ragnarok (2022)

God of War: Ragnarok is an action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It serves as a sequel to the 2018 game God of War and follows Kratos and his son Atreus as they face the looming threat of Ragnarok.

Main Characters

  • Kratos - The Ghost of Sparta and main protagonist.
  • Atreus - Kratos' son, also known as Loki.
  • Thor - The Norse God of Thunder, a major antagonist.
  • Odin - The All-Father, ruling over Asgard.
  • Freya - A former ally turned foe, seeking vengeance.

Reviews

God of War: Ragnarok has received critical acclaim, praised for its storytelling, gameplay mechanics, and character development. It holds a high rating across various review platforms.
